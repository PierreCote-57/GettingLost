Hello. This humble site is a combination resource and journal of our journey. We are Joy (retired) and Pierre (almost retired) and just want to enjoy the beautiful Vancouver Island where we live. To achieve that goal, we decided to purchase a camper van and hit the road.

Throughout, you will see several destination pages as well as blog posts documenting our journey. There is already tons of valuable information on the web, some by the British Columbia governement as well as many from private citizen (like us). The goal is not to rewrite what has already been written, rather to provide a central place from which these great resources can be found without google. As such, a lake page may include links to BC parks, a map of rec site showing the campsites a link to a external reservation site and/or a link to the lake chart (published by BC's Ministry of The Environment). By providing links to these resources, we also acknowledge the work they have done to provide the information and let them have the credit.
