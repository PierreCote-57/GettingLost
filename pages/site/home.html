The best journeys have no plan.

A personal travel journal from the roads less taken



There's a certain magic that only happens when you stop following the itinerary. When the map folds the wrong way, when you take the road that doesn't have a name, when you sit down at a table and can't read the menu — that's when the real journey begins.

This is where I keep those moments. The wrong turns that became the best turns. The places I didn't mean to find.
