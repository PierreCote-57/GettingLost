Beautiful, small lake with easy access and only 30 minutes from Campbell River. The park at the South end is access through a small dirt road, has a bathroom (possibly dirty), picnic table, boat ramp and a great fishing pier. There is also access directly from I-28, through small access path, that is easy to miss as you drive through.

Ideal place for taking the young ones. This is also my goto site to test gear, small boat, new casting rod or to practice with your fly rod. Fishing is likely to be hit/miss, be aware of the stocking (plant) dates (see below). You are much more likely to catch small trout than the lunker you wanted to brag about.

Getting there is straight forward, beware of the sharp left tur when exiting I-28. The picnic/fishing area is 5 minutes from I-28, on a wide, bumpy dirt road; the entrance is easily visible on the left.

The main area has a picnic table, fire pit as well as a beautiful fishing pier. There is also a surprisingly good boat ramp. Remember that only electric motors are allowed on this small lake.
