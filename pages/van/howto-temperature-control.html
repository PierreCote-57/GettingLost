The details

The van comes with both heat and AC, and the temperature control can be fully automated.

The # 1 thing to understand is that the Tuma panel is in charge. At best, all the Garmin panel can do is send settings to the Tuma panel,

There are three elements at play for the complete system:

  1. Where the power comes from
  2. The Truma control (set to external control) — see , ,
  3. The Garmin control panel, the brains — see ,

The eventual goal is to control the heater and the AC, each of which has separate power requirements and options

  1. The heater can use
    1. electricity
    2. gas
    3. a mix of both
  2. The AC (electric only) can use
    1. Use 120 VAC
    2. Requires shore power or generator
    3. Will not start on battery alone
