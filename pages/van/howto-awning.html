Instructions

To extend

  1. Look at the Awning control panel, located just to the right of the Garmin panel
  2. Make sure the left toggle is set to ON
  3. Press (no need to hold) extend on the right switch
  4. The awning will now extend

To retract

  1. Look at the , located just to the right of the Garmin panel
  2. Make sure the left toggle is set to ON
  3. Press (no need to hold) retract on the right switch
  4. The awning will now extend
