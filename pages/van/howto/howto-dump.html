How to: Empty water tanks

It's a dirty job, but somebody's got to do it!

Getting ready
  1. Have on hand
    • The sewer hose & fittings
    • A fresh-water hose
    • Disposable gloves
    • Hand sanitizer
  2. Drive to the dump station and position the van so the sewer outlet lines up with the station's drain.
  3. Set the parking brake.

At the station
  1. Remove the sewer cap from the outlet.
  2. Connect the sewer hose: one end to the van's outlet, the other secured into the dump station drain.
  3. Open the black water valve first and let it fully drain. Draining black first keeps the worst waste moving through the hose while it's cleanest.
  4. Close the black water valve once it stops flowing.
  5. Open the gray water valve and let it fully drain — the gray water rinses out residual solids left behind by the black tank.
  6. Close the gray water valve.
  7. Flush the black tank using the factory flush port: connect a fresh-water hose to the port, open the black valve again, and run water until it runs clear, then close the valve.
  8. Rinse the sewer hose with a dump station rinse hose (or your fresh-water hose) while it's still connected, to flush out residue.
  9. Disconnect the hose starting from the tank end, letting any residual liquid drain into the dump station opening.
  10. Rinse the outlet cap and hose fittings, then recap the sewer outlet.
