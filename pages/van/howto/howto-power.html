Managing power

Every system you use in the van needs power. And, Unlike at your home, power is limited and must be managed. Some systems provide you with the power, some use said power. The trick is to match the two.

Power consumption — raw

Draw for each element on its own — the building blocks. The Power column is what the element runs on natively; battery is shown as instantaneous draw (A) and, where the source figure is a nightly total, accumulated use (Ah). Where a load has an average and a peak, the average is shown and the peak is noted.

120 VAC loads are tricky: on shore power or the generator they cost no battery, but off-grid they pull from the battery through the inverter — so a battery draw is shown for them too. Figures marked typ. are computed from rated wattage (battery A ≈ watts ÷ ~11) and still need measuring.
