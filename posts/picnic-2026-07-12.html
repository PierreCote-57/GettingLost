5:00 PM update

We are back. That was an adventure. Driving the forestry road with the van, at this point setup for normal roads, is very different than driving a Jeep Trail Rated with AT tires. The big conclusion from today is that the van, in its current configuration, will not go back on forestry roads.

The learning experience taught us

  1. The van's suspension + tires is setup for road
  2. We want to keep the van as is until the trip to New Fouddland
  3. Until (if) suspension and tires are upgraded, the van stays on normal roads
  4. The trip to artic requires the upgrades

Other than the road, the site was nice, beautiful fishing pier, nice boat ramp (a bit steep), several picnic tables & fire pits and a BC-standard bathroom.
